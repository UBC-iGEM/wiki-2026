# Purpose

Pick a single colony and grow it up in liquid culture to miniprep and send it for sequencing.

# Materials & Methods

Transformed DH5a single colonies from [Notebook Entry 2](/wet-lab/lab-notebook-2026/notebook-entry-2)
5 mL of LB media (per single colony) containing 17 mg/L of Chloramphenicol (Cm) antibiotic

1. Circle 1-2 single isolated colonies.
2. Dip the colonies in their separate tubes of 5 mL Cm containing media.
3. Incubate the colonies at 37°C while shaking overnight.

# Results

The colonies successfully grew overnight in the liquid culture.

# Summary

Single colonies of recA or CpxP plasmid-containing bacteria were successfully inoculated and grew in liquid culture overnight.

Next steps are to miniprep the liquid culture and send it off for sequencing.
